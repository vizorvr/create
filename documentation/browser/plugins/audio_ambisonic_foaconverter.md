#FOAmbisonic converter

##Description
Decode an ambisonic spherical recording to headphones.

##Inputs
###audio_in
A 4-channel B-format audio stream to convert.

###convention
Ambisonic convention:
**0**: ACN/N3D
**1**: WXYZ/FuMa
**2**: ACN/SN3D.

##Outputs
###audio_out
A converted 4-channel B-format audio stream.
