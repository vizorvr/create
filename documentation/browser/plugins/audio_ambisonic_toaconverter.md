#TOAmbisonic converter

##Description
Decode an ambisonic spherical recording to headphones.

##Inputs
###audio_in
A 16-channel TOA audio stream to convert.

###convention
Ambisonic convention:<br>
**0**: ACN/N3D<br>
**1**: WXYZ/FuMa<br>
**2**: ACN/SN3D.

##Outputs
###audio_out
A converted 16-channel TOA audio stream.
