#FOAmbisonic mirror

##Description
Mirror an ambisonic spherical recording across the principal planes.

##Inputs
###audio_in
A 4-channel B-format audio stream.

###mirror_plane
Plane to mirror:<br>
**0**: None<br>
**1**: Front-Back<br>
**2**: Left-Right<br>
**3**: Up-Down

##Outputs
###audio_out
A mirrored 4-channel B-format audio stream.
