#TOAmbisonic mirror

##Description
Mirror an ambisonic spherical recording across the principal planes.

##Inputs
###audio_in
A 16-channel TOA audio stream.

###mirror_plane
Plane to mirror:<br>
**0**: None<br>
**1**: Front-Back<br>
**2**: Left-Right<br>
**3**: Up-Down.<br>

##Outputs
###audio_out
A mirrored 16-channel TOA audio stream.
